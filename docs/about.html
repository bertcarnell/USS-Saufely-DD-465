About the Author

Robert Carnell

I am the grandson of Warrant Gunner Robert Rettgers who served on the USS SAUFLEY in WWII. I am also a graduate of the US Naval Academy, serving as a submarine officer on the USS LOUISIANNA and as a Naval ROTC instructor at Miami University.

The primary goal this website was to determine how much information could be extracted from the digitized war reports of the SAUFLEY, but it has grown into a location where I can share everything that I have found about the war service of the ship and its crew.
